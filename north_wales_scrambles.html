North Wales Scrambles Map

The guidebook to 50 scrambles in North Wales by Garry Smith (ISBN 978-0-9929692-0-2) lacks an overview map showing the location of all the scrambles contained within the guide. I've had a go at making a pdf pull-out-and-keep map that should fit within a copy of the guidebook, when folded in half. Don't scale the pdf to your printer paper otherwise it won't fit. There are two flavours, one using Stamen Maps, and one using Mapbox. The colours (green/blue/red) refer to grade 1/2/3 scrambles, as coloured in the guidebook.

Let me know what you think.
