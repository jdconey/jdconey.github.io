Scottish Winter Webcam Picture Archive

Pictures from on the hour, every hour from 8 am until 4 pm GMT, 1st Nov until 1st May.

Cairngorms | Glencoe & Crianlarich | The Ben | Nevis Range ski centre

This idea sprung about on a very relaxed wander into the CIC hut with some pals from my uni hillwalking club in the warmth of January 2020. We always found that when we wanted to check the webcams in Scotland of an evening after uni/ work for an idea of snow conditions on the hill, it was dark. So this hopefully bridges this gap by allowing an archive of images to be stored for future perusal in the hours of darkness. Hopefully, some folk, somewhere, may find it of use for looking at snow conditions through the week.
