Scottish Winter Webcam Picture Archive

Pictures from on the hour, every hour from 8 am until 4 pm GMT, 1st Nov until 1st May.

This idea sprung about on a very relaxed wander into the CIC hut with some pals from my uni hillwalking club in the warmth of January 2020. One of us mentioned that whenever we wanted to check the webcams in Scotland of an evening after uni/work for an idea of snow conditions on the hill, it was nighttime so (surprisingly enough) the current webcam images were not of much use. This is aimed as a potential solution, allowing anyone to have a gander at snow conditions on the hill during daylight hours at any time of the day. This is via the means of the various webcams that various generous companies and folk have installed. This is also useful for those of us who don't do any prep for a scottish winter weekend so can have a mooch on the way up. Hopefully, some folk, somewhere, may find it of use for looking at snow conditions through the week. Please don't sue me.
