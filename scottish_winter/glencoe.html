Also worth checking out is the Pier House Webcam of the Pap etc (sadly it's a video stream and I'm not clever enough to grab images from it). The Traffic Scotland webcams at Altnafeadh (East and West) may also be of use. Unfortunately Traffic Scotland don't like folk scraping images. Boo.

The Oban Times has a webcam at Loch Etive pointing at Ben Cruachan. Webcam views are allegedly limited to 5 devices at one time, for up to a minute each. On each time I've tried to view the webcam, I've received nowt except very large ads. So proceed with caution.
